About GDD Motors

Your Trusted Vehicle Partner in Botswana

Founded in 2010, GDD Motors has grown to become one of Botswana's leading automotive dealerships, offering premium new and pre-owned vehicles along with exceptional after-sales service.

Our History

2010

Foundation

GDD Motors was established in Gaborone with a small showroom and service center.

2014

Expansion

We expanded our operations to include a second location in Francistown.

2018

Award Winning

Recognized as Botswana's "Dealership of the Year" by the Automotive Association.

2023

New Facilities

Opened our state-of-the-art service center with 15 service bays in Gaborone.
