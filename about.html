About GDD Motors

Our History

Founded in 1995, GDD Motors has been serving the community for nearly 30 years. What started as a small family-owned dealership has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers.

At GDD Motors, our mission is simple: to provide exceptional vehicles and service at fair prices. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers based on trust and transparency.
